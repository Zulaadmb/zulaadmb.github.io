Few Facts about Camels in Mongolian Gobi Desert

  • Camels can travel without drinking water for months
  • Camels are very hairy though survive during winter
  • Most of the time the camels move slowly, but, if pressed, they can run at speeds of over 60 miles per hour
  • Camels live for 20 to 40 years with some living as long as 50 years
